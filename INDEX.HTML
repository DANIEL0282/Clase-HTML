- hr Kings of Leon is an American rock band that formed in Nashville, Tennessee, in 1999. The band consists of three brothers, Caleb (lead vocals, guitar), Nathan (drums, percussion), and Jared Followill (bass, keyboards), and their cousin Matthew Followill (guitar). The band's musical style is rooted in Southern rock, blues rock, and garage rock. They have released eight studio albums,
with their debut album, Youth and Young Manhood, being released in 2003. The album was a critical and commercial success, and it helped to establish the band as one of the leading bands of the new Southern rock movement. Kings of Leon have won numerous awards, including four Grammy Awards, three Brit Awards, and an MTV Video Music Award.
They have also been nominated for two Academy Awards. The band has sold over 20 million albums worldwide, making them one of the most successful rock bands of the 21st century. In addition to their music, Kings of Leon are also known for their live performances. They are known for their energetic shows and their ability to connect with their audience.
